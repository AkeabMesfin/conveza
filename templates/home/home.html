    What is Conveza?

    Conveza is a platform designed for entrepreneurs to connect, collaborate, and grow their businesses. It offers mentorship, partnership opportunities, and networking with like-minded individuals.

    How do I find a mentor?

    You can find a mentor by participating in our community. Post your questions, engage in group chats, and use direct messaging to connect with potential mentors who can offer the guidance you need.

    How can I find a business partner?

    To find a business partner, use our platform’s features to engage with others. Post about your needs, join relevant group chats, and connect through direct messaging to discover potential partners who share your goals.
